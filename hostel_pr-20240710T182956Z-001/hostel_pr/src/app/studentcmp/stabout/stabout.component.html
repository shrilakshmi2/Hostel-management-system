Hostel Rules

1. Quiet Hours: Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Please keep noise levels down during these times.
2. No Visitors: Visitors are not allowed in the hostel rooms.
3. Smoking: Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the hostel premises.
4. Cleanliness: Keep your room and common areas clean at all times.
5. Laundry: Use the designated laundry area for washing clothes.
6. Kitchen Use: If you use the kitchen, clean up after yourself and label your food items.
7. Guests: Notify hostel management in advance if you plan to have guests staying overnight.
8. Fire Safety: Familiarize yourself with fire escape routes and fire safety procedures.
9. Respect Others: Respect the privacy and belongings of your fellow residents.
10. Compliance: Failure to comply with hostel rules may result in disciplinary action or eviction.
